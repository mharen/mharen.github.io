Simple Photo Sheet Printer

Batch print your photos.

Simple Photo Sheet Printer lets you choose several photos, and send a tiled sheet straight to AirPrint or a compatible printer. Everything happens on-device.

Supported devices iPhone and iPad on iOS/iPadOS 18 or later.
Print options Choose tile size, paper size, and fit or fill placement.
Privacy No analytics, no accounts, no uploads, no third-party tracking.
Contact Support

Support

For questions, bug reports, or App Store support requests, email image-printer@wassupy.com. Include your device model, iOS or iPadOS version, and the print size you were trying to use so we can reproduce the issue quickly.

Privacy

Simple Photo Sheet Printer is designed to work entirely on your device. The app requests read-only access to the photos you choose so it can prepare a printable page layout. It does not request or use location data, does not analyze your photo library beyond your selection, and does not transmit your images or personal information anywhere.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, sign-in systems, or remote storage. Printed pages are generated locally and sent only to the system print sheet you control.
