Support

Step Totals is an iPhone app that reads your Apple Health step count and walking or running distance so you can review daily totals and totals across a chosen date range.

Supported devices

iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later.

How to use the app

  • Open the app and allow read access to Apple Health data when prompted.
  • Select a start date and end date, or use a quick range shortcut.
  • Review totals for days, steps, miles, and the daily breakdown list.

Troubleshooting

  • If no data appears, confirm that your iPhone has Apple Health step or walking/running distance data.
  • If access was denied, reopen Apple Health or iPhone Settings and re-enable Health access for Step Totals.
  • HealthKit data is only available on a physical iPhone, not the iOS Simulator.
