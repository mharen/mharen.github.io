Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Step Totals is an iPhone app that reads Apple Health step count and walking or running distance data so you can view daily totals and totals across a selected date range.

Information Step Totals accesses

  • Step count from Apple Health
  • Walking or running distance from Apple Health

How the data is used

Step Totals uses this data only to display fitness summaries inside the app. The app does not use Health data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or resale.

Data collection and sharing

Step Totals does not require an account. The app does not collect your Health data on a remote server, does not sell your data, and does not share Health data with third parties.

Data retention

Step Totals reads Apple Health data from your device to calculate summaries. Health data stays on your device and is not stored by the developer on external systems.

Your choices

You can deny or revoke Health access at any time through Apple Health or iPhone Settings. If access is revoked, Step Totals will no longer be able to show Health-based summaries.

Children

Step Totals is a general fitness utility and is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
