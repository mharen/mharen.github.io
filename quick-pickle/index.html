Apple Watch App

Minimal pickleball scoring on your wrist.

Quick Pickle is a compact Apple Watch scorekeeper built for active games. It keeps score, shows the current service turn, and makes it easy to correct mistakes without digging through menus.

Get Support Read Privacy Policy
What It Does

Made for fast score calls

  • Tracks the current score in standard pickleball call format.
  • Shows the active serving team and service-turn state.
  • Keeps recent history visible for quick corrections mid-game.
Support

Need help?

If you run into a bug, have a rules question, or want to request a feature, email quick-pickle@wassupy.com.

  • Please include your watchOS version and a short description of the issue.
  • If possible, include the exact game state where the issue happened.
We don't track anything

Privacy Policy

Quick Pickle does not require an account and does not collect personal information from you inside the app.

  • The app is designed to run locally on your Apple Watch.
  • Game scores and recent history are used only for in-app functionality.
  • Quick Pickle does not sell your personal data.
  • Quick Pickle does not include advertising or third-party analytics inside the app.
  • If you contact support by email, your email and message will be used only to respond to your request.
